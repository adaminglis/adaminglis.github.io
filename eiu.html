Economist Intelegence Unit

Who are the EIU?

The Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU) is the research and analysis division of The Economist Group, and the sister company to The Economist newspaper.

The EIU helps businesses, the financial sector, and governments, understand how the world is changing and how that creates opportunities to be seized and risks to be managed.

As the research lead, I wanted to know what was needed to begin prototyping a dedicated content-driven app that could increase overall engagement in the EIU’s free marketing content.

Research outline

Objective

Find new ways to increase engagement with the EIU through sign-ups, views, downloads, and sharing.

Goals

  1. Create a companion app for the Insights section of the EIU website.
  2. Create opportunities for increased engagement of users through:
    • Increased sign-ups, views, downloads, and sharing;
    • Increased loyalty through use over time, and retention;
    • Increased numbers of inquiries.
  3. Design a mobile experience that encourages users to engage with EIU outside of an office environment/work setting.
  4. Create opportunities for users’ onward journey.
  5. Consider various types of digital media content that may include: videography, podcasts, photography, illustrations, interactive and animated elements, maps and geo-visualizations, information graphics, data visualizations, whitepaper documents, etc.
Research questions

  • How can we introduce new users to the EIU brand?
  • What are the current user’s needs?
  • What would keep users coming back to an EIU app?
  • How can we scale EIU insights section into a mobile experience?
  • How can we create a clear path to conversion for EIU customers?
